When to use this service

Update an existing title

Use this service for applications to update the whole of a registered title or for interests (other than a charge) affecting part of a registered title. This service is limited to applying for simple transactions like amendments, notices and restrictions.

Don't use this service:

  • for transfers of part (TP1 and TP2) or assents of part (AS3)
  • to register a deed of variation where the deed attempts to (a) vary the length of a registered lease by increasing the term, or (b) add new land to the extent demised by a registered lease - see Practice Guide 28 (link opens in a new window)
  • when the grant of a new lease takes effect as a surrender of an existing registered lease by operation of law

You can make these applications using the Document registration service (link opens in a new window).
